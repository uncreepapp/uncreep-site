Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Waitlist & contact data: your email address when you join the waitlist (processed via Formspree) or email us.
  • Purchase data: handled by our merchant of record, Dodo Payments. We receive your email and purchase status — never your card details.
  • App data: the current demo runs entirely in your browser; scope items and client messages you paste are processed locally and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Basic analytics: aggregate, non-identifying usage statistics from our hosting provider (Vercel).

How we use it

To provide the Service, deliver purchases, respond to support requests, and send occasional product updates (you can unsubscribe anytime). We do not sell your personal data.

Third parties

We rely on: Vercel (hosting), Formspree (waitlist form), Dodo Payments (checkout, as merchant of record), and Google (email). Each processes data under its own privacy policy.

Data retention & rights

Email us to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. We keep waitlist emails until you unsubscribe or ask for deletion.
